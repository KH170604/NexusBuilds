01

Configure Your Build

Start off with our dual-mode online configurator. For those who are familiar with PC configuration, you'll feel right at home with the advanced mode. For those who are less familiar, don't worry! We've cut out all the technical mumbo jumbo in the beginner mode, so you can tell us what you want and we'll handle the rest.

  • check_circle Real-time compatibility validation for advanced users
  • check_circle Simple ask-and-receive workflow for beginners
  • check_circle Live customer support for both beginners and advanced users
02

Expert Review

Once you submit your build request ticket, a dedicated Nexus engineer will review every detail. We make sure that your build is optimized to fit both your workflow and your desired aesthetics. Tickets will be processed within 3 business days, so you can expect to hear back from us as soon as possible.

  • check_circle Manual review by experienced builders
  • check_circle Expert component sourcing
  • check_circle One-on-one consultations via phone or messaging for post-submission changes

03

Quotation & Consultation

After your ticket has been processed, we offer a one-on-one consultation with your builder to verify your final build. We'll discuss possible add-ons, verify budget adjustments, and confirm your build is ready for production.

  • check_circle Guaranteed pricing for 48 hours.
  • check_circle Detailed component list with brand and model specifics.
  • check_circle Estimated build and delivery timeline provided.
04

Approve & Order

After consultation and your approval, you'll receive a final quotation. This document locks in the price of your components for 48 hours, protecting you from market volatility. We ensure total transparency. We don't do hidden assembly fees or last-minute surcharges. As soon as your payment is processed, your build enters our production queue.

  • check_circle Instant component allocation to secure hardware.
  • check_circle Client Dashboard access for live tracking.
  • check_circle Dedicated build slot reservation.

05

Product Assembly

Building times will vary depending on build complexity, but rest assured that your PC and/or PC setups are in good hands. Most of our builders have been building PCs since middle school, and for ultra-tier builds with custom-made components, we even have designers on call to make sure your builds are true showpieces.

  • check_circle Professional cable management for airflow and aesthetics.
  • check_circle BIOS update and optimization for stability.
  • check_circle Photo updates sent to your dashboard during the build.
06

Delivery & Warranty

We offer both in-store pickup and shipping courtesy of UPS. All standard builds come with a 6-month warranty, with advanced-tier builds receiving a 1-year warranty and ultra-tier builds receiving 2-years. Never hesitate to reach out to us if you have any questions or concerns after your build has been shipped!
